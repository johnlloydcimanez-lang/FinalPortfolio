what is Bulalo?

Bulalo is a traditional Filipino soup made with beef shanks and bone marrow, slow-simmered to create a rich, flavorful broth.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs beef shank (with bone marrow), cut into chunks
  • 1 small bundle Chinese cabbage (pechay)
  • 1 small bundle bok choy
  • 1 small head cabbage, quartered
  • 1 medium onion, quartered or sliced
  • 6-7 cups water
  • 2 pieces white or yellow corn, cut in halves
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce (patis)
  • 1 tbsp whole peppercorns
  • Salt to taste

Step-by-Step Process

  1. In a pot, simmer the beef shank for at least an hour or until tender.
  2. Remove the scum that rises above the soup. Add more hot water if necessary.
  3. When beef is fork-tender, add the onion, salt, whole peppercorn, and corn. Let stand for 5 - 8 minutes.
  4. Add the cabbage, Chinese cabbage, and bok choy. Seasons with fish sauce according to taste.
  5. Remove from heat and serve it until it's hot for a better taste.
